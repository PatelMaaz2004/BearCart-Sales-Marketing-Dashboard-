## 📌 Project Overview

**BearCart: E-Commerce Growth & Conversion Intelligence** was developed during **Hack4Insights 2025**, a hackathon organized by **ITM Dataminds Community, ITM Kharghar** on **18 December 2025**.

The objective of this challenge was to analyze a complex e-commerce dataset, identify key business opportunities, and develop an interactive Business Intelligence solution that helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

The project involved extensive data preprocessing, data integration, business analysis, dashboard development, and presentation of actionable insights.

# 🎯 Problem Statement

The challenge was to transform multiple raw datasets into meaningful business intelligence by answering critical business questions related to:

- Revenue Performance
- Marketing Effectiveness
- Customer Conversion
- Traffic Sources
- Refund Analysis
- Customer Behaviour
- Business Growth Opportunities

---

# 📂 Dataset

The dataset was provided by the university as part of the hackathon.

### Dataset Characteristics

- 📁 7 CSV Files
- 📊 Multiple interconnected datasets
- 🧹 Significant data cleaning required
- 🔄 Data integration across multiple sources
- 📈 Business-ready dataset created after preprocessing

One of the most challenging parts of this project was the **data cleaning and integration process**. Considerable time was spent identifying inconsistencies, handling missing values, standardizing formats, merging datasets, and preparing a clean analytical model before any dashboard development could begin.

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

The final solution consists of two interactive Power BI dashboards.

## 1️⃣ Revenue Performance Dashboard

This dashboard focuses on the financial performance of BearCart.

### Key KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Net Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Revenue Trend
- Refund Analysis
- Traffic Source Analysis

### Business Insights

- Revenue trend across different periods
- Top-performing traffic sources
- Revenue impact of refunds
- Order value analysis
- Business growth opportunities

---

## 2️⃣ Marketing Effectiveness Dashboard

### Business Insights

- Device-wise customer behaviour
